Apply

Applications for Summer 2020 will be available on March 1, 2020.

Tentative Timeline

  • Completed applications are due Monday, March 30, 2020.
  • Recommendation letters are also due Monday, March 30, 2020. You will enter information about your letter writer on the application form, and we will contact them after you submit your application. It is strongly recommended that you contact your letter writer ahead of time to make sure that their letter arrives on time.
  • Notification of acceptance will be sent out Monday, April 20, 2020.
  • If you are accepted, you will have one week to confirm your intent to attend.

Admission Criteria

Students must be in 9-12th grade at the time of application.

Ideal Candidates

Ideal candidates should:
  • Have an interest in AI (no previous AI background is required).
  • Have an interest in biology or medicine.
  • Have leadership skills and the potential to make a broader impact.
  • Be passionate about creating an inclusive and diverse environment.

Application Checklist

  • A school transcript or grade report — unofficial transcripts are acceptable. Provide the most recent year of grades.
  • Recommendation letter — on the application, you will be able to include the contact information of your recommendation letter writer. We will contact them after you submit your application. Required information: write full name, email address, phone number

Tuition

The program is free for the students.

Questions?

If you have any questions regarding the application process, please contact ai4all@listsrv.ucsf.edu

About Us

AI4ALL is a US nonprofit dedicated to increasing diversity and inclusion in AI education, research, development, and policy. Click here to learn more about all of the AI4ALL summer programs.

UCSF AI4ALL is a summer program run in partnership between UCSF and AI4ALL, staffed by UCSF professors and students who want to share their passion for AI with a diverse next generation.
